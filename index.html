Welcome to the Blabla Magazine!

Are you a puppy lover always in search of Mormon gossip and from time to time believe that abstaining from reproduction is the only possible way to prevent environmental degradation? You are in the right place.

The Blabla Magazine is a digital collection of nine of the most interesting articles you could find on the web about Mormonism, Voluntary Human Extinction Movement and pets (not related unfortunately!). The articles are organised in three columns, in order to let you read and compare them at the same time and individually, thanks to the independent scrollbars. You can even choose to get rid of one or two articles and read the one you prefer to select. The visualisation is enhanced by the possibility to choose between different layouts: before 1800, 19th century, first and second half of the 20th century, 21st century and futuristic styles. This way you will discover what fonts, decorations, styles and colours were (or supposed to be) used in magazines of different historical periods.

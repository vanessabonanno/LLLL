Welcome to Nostalgia magazine

Are you a puppy lover always in search of Mormon gossip and from time to time believe that abstaining from reproduction is the only possible way to prevent environmental degradation? You are in the right place.

Nostalgia is a digital collection of nine of the most interesting articles you could find on the web that explore humanity's obsession with the past and future.

Three issues— Mormons, Pets, and the Voluntary Human Extinction Movement— examine how identities and realities are shaped through memories of the past and imagined futures.

Six viewing styles—Preprint, 19th C., Art Nouveau, Sixties, Terminal, and Future—experiment with notions of tradition, innovation, and nostalgia in media.

Tools

On the left, you will find the metadata viewer, a powerful tool which will help you explore the issue.

The first section, Articles allows you to choose which articles to show on your device. Useful shortcuts will lead to every subheading of every article, you only need to select one and click!

Below, the Editorial Metadata section allows you to show or hide the author, date of publication, source, and other editorial information.

Finally there is the Content Metadata. Here you can search for key players, places and concepts, in one or more articles.
